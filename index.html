About Us

Fairmount Hill Child Care is a home daycare specializing in infants and toddlers. We keep our ratios low - 1:2 for infants, 1:3 for toddlers. We provide everything - milk, formula, home-cooked food, diapers, wipes, and bedding. All you need to pack are spare clothes. We're open 8-6, M-F, and offer full- or half-time options.

We make learning fun, with plenty of opportunities for free and guided play. Your child will be exposed to music, dance, imaginative play, cross-cultural learning, baby sign language, books, books, and more books! Toddlers enjoy daily sensory learning activities, and special, age-appropriate activities, working hands-on with art, science, cognitive learning, fine motor manipulatives, reading and math readiness. We take advantage of beautiful days with trips to the playground or to the splash deck in summer, and even in cold weather we try to get outside for a quick burst of fresh air.

Sample Schedules

FAIRMOUNT HILL CHILD CARE Sample Infant Schedule

You can download a PDF copy of this sample schedule here

Young infants maintain their own personal schedule and are constantly supervised during nap time for the first six weeks. They move gradually to the infant schedule when they are ready. Diapers are changed when needed, but no less than three times per day. We provide all the diapers, wipes, formula, and homemade baby food unless parents wish to supply their own.

8am infants are fed as they arrive, then have time to explore the playroom
8:45 circle time: songs, sign language word of the day, book of the day
9am free play
9:30 nap time
11am diaper change. Lunch time.
11:30 outside time
12:30 individualized play and age-appropriate learning activities
1:30 storytime
1:45 diaper change. Milk
2pm nap time
4pm individualized play and age-appropriate learning activities
5pm diaper change. Dinner time.
5:30 free play and clean-up
6pm end of day

FAIRMOUNT HILL CHILD CARE Sample Toddler Schedule

You can download a PDF copy of this sample schedule here

Young toddlers are encouraged to take a morning nap at 9:30, but drop it generally around 18 months, when they’re ready to join us for morning learning activities. Diapers are changed when needed, but no less than three times per day. We provide all the diapers, wipes, milk and home-cooked food unless parents wish to supply their own.

8am arrival and free play, listen to our classical composer of the month
8:45 circle time: songs, sign language word of the day, book of the day
9am breakfast
9:30 toddler library – here we work on fine motor skills, art, science, or cooking projects, and review our book of the day. When feasible, we borrow activity ideas from the book, and eat the foods that appear in the book at meal and snack times as a way of bringing the book to life.
10am guided learning – gross motor play, dance, imaginative play, etc.
10:30 diaper change. Outside play
11:30 free play
12pm lunch time
12:30 free play
1pm sensory learning – painting, water table, bubbles, etc.
1:30 flash cards, storytime
1:45 diaper change. Milk for young toddlers. Tooth brushing for all
2pm nap time
4pm snack time. At least once a week, we use this time for cultural learning. We learn about one country per month, and at this time we’ll find it on our globe, listen to popular music from the country, read a picture book, watch a short video of kids dancing, play games, eat snacks, etc.
4:30 guided learning – imaginative play, fine motor, counting activities, sorting, categorizing etc.
5pm diaper change. Free play
5:30 clean-up
6pm end of day
